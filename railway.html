RAILWAY 2024 EXAM DETAILS

RRB JE Recruitment 2024

RRB Junior Engineer Recruitment is one of the most awaited vacancies. Candidates with Engineering Diploma and Degree are eligible for the JE Recruitment. The vacancy notice for the JE will be released in July. It is expected that there will be more than 10000 vacancies to be filled up.

  • Vacancy Name – RRB NTPC Recruitment
  • Number of Vacancies – 20000+ (Notice Awaited)
  • Educational Qualification – Graduate and Undergraduate
  • Age Limit – 18 to 33 Years
  • Starting Date to apply – June 2024 (Final Notice Awaited)

Selection of the candidates will be done based on CBT and Skill Test. Candidates will participate in Level 1 and Level 2 CBT and then in the Skill Test. Examination for the NTPC posts is likely to be conducted in September 2024.

RRB Paramedical Recruitment 2024 Notice

Paramedical Staff Recruitment includes vacancies like Staff Nurses, Health Inspectors, Lab Technicians, Lab Assistants, pharmacists, and Others. RRB is expected to release notice for more than 4000 posts this year. The candidates waiting for this vacancy should note the notice will be released in July 2024. Once notice is released, they can apply online according to the preferred zones.

  • Vacancy Name – RRB NTPC Recruitment
  • Number of Vacancies – 20000+ (Notice Awaited)
  • Educational Qualification – Graduate and Undergraduate
  • Age Limit – 18 to 33 Years
  • Starting Date to apply – June 2024 (Final Notice Awaited)
